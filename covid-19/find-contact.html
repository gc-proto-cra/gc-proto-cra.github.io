Find the right CRA contact or telephone number

Answer a few questions to find the telephone number or contact you need.

Which of these are you calling for?

  • Personal

    What do you want to call about?

    • My Account, NETFILE, Represent a Client, mobile apps
    • Change personal information (address, name)
    • Change direct deposit/bank information
    • A benefit or credit

      What benefit do you want to call about?

      • Canada Child Benefit (CCB) or Alberta Child Benefit
      • Quebec Family allowance for children (Retraite Canada)
      • GST/HST credit or Disability Benefit
      • Receiving Employment Insurance (EI), Canada Pension Plan (CPP) or Old Age Security (OAS)
      • An Ontario benefit
      • An Alberta benefit
      • Air Travellers' Security Charge refunds
      • Get a tax document, form or publication

        What kind of document do you need to get?

        • A Notice of Assessment or Reassessment
        • An official proof of income (option C print)
        • A remittance voucher
        • A tax form or publication
      • Other benefits
    • Check the status of your refund
    • Get a Notice of Assessment, receipt of payment (remittance voucher), proof of income, form, publication

      What kind of document do you need to get?

      • A Notice of Assessment or Reassessment
      • An official proof of income (option C print)
      • A remittance voucher
      • A tax form or publication
    • Problem making a payment

      What kind of payment problem do you want to fix?

      • Can't make a payment (make a payment arrangement)
      • Missing or misallocated payment
      • Already late on a payment
      • Double payment of social security because of working internationally
      • Follow up on a Requirements To Pay (RTP) form you received
    • Completing personal income taxes

      What do you want to call about?

      • How to report income on your personal income tax return
      • Issues with filing for the first time
      • Order an income tax package
      • Get help with Phoenix pay system related issues
      • Income Tax Rulings Directorate
    • Registered savings plans or Tax-Free Savings Account (RRSP, RESP, TFSA) and Home Buyers Plan

      What do you want to call about?

      • Find out your TFSA or RRSP contribution room
      • Questions about the Home Buyers Plan (HBP)
      • Get help with registering or questions about an RRSP
      • None of the above
    • Check your tax account balance
    • Make an appeal or objection
    • Trusts and estates
    • Taxes for non residents (Part XIII)
    • None of the above
  • Business or self-employed

    What do you want to call about?

    • My Business Account, Represent a Client, Payroll Deductions Online, mobile apps
    • Get an access code

      For which kinds of information returns does your business need an access code?

      • GST/HST
      • Information returns
    • Filing returns (NETFILE, TELEFILE, EFILE, T2 filing)

      What filing service do you need help with?

      • GST/HST NETFILE and TELEFILE, Information returns (such as T3, T4 or T5 slips)
      • EFILE
      • Corporation Internet Filing for T2s
      • Assistance completing a return
    • Change business information (address, name, direct deposit/bank information)
    • Problem making a payment

      What kind of payment problem do you want to fix?

      • Can't make a payment (make a payment arrangement)
      • Missing or misallocated payment
      • Already late on a payment
      • Questions on corporation instalments (when required to pay towards an estimate as they go)
    • Business credits and allowances

      What kind of credit do you want help with?

      • Film Production Tax Credit
      • Scientific Research and Experimental Development (SRED)
      • Register or get help with the Children's Special Allowances (CSA)
      • Other business credits
    • Get a business number or program account number
    • Report a nil remittance

      What is the nil remittance for?

      • Payroll
      • Non-resident withholding tax
    • GST/HST, excise and duties, Air Travellers' Security Charge

      What do you want to call about?

      • Softwood lumber

        What do you want to call about?

        • Rates for softwood lumber export charges
        • Check your softwood lumber export balance and other help
      • GST/HST

        What do you want to call about?

        • Charging GST/HST as a business (including rebates such as the new housing rebates)
        • Get a GST/HST number
        • Call when you received a letter for GST/HST post assessing
        • Make a GST/HST appeal or objection
        • Ask for a ruling or interpretation on the GST/HST or one of the First Nations taxes
        • Financial institutions' questions about technical issues with the GST/HST
      • For a ruling, interpretation or technical enquiry on excise duty
      • Excise taxes, duties and other levies

    • Make an appeal or objection
    • Flow-through Shares
    • Discounter services
    • Electronic Processing Unit
    • Other help with payroll or business taxes

  • Report a scam or tax fraud

    • Report a lead on offshore tax cheating

Change your GST/HST Access Code

Online:

GST/HST Access Code Online lets you change your access code to a number of your choice. You will need your business number and some information from a previously filed return.

Call us to change your GST/HST Access Code

To verify your identity, you'll need

  • Your business number
  • Your business name
  • Your complete business address
