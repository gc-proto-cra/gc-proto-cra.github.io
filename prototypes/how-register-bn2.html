Content for Non-Resident

BN - Business number

Personal information:

  • Last name
  • Social insurance number (SIN)
  • Date of birth
  • Personal postal code

If you are a representative, you will need to provide your RepID. For more information on representatives, go to Represent a Client.

Basic business information:

  • Business name
  • Type of business (for example: sole proprietor, partnership, corporation, domestic worker, trust)
  • Name and SIN of owners as required
  • Physical address
  • Mailing address (if different from the physical address)
  • Description of major business activity

BNNR - Non-res business number

Information you need for registration:

  • Name and title
  • Work telephone number
  • Information for the primary point of contact if different
  • Business contact information, including business name and address
  • Description of the main activity of the business in Canada
  • Main products and services provided by the business
  • Mailing address and books and records address, if different from the physical address
  • Business owners information, including name and title and work telephone number (and SIN?)

RT - GST/HST program account

You must have all of the following on hand:

  • amount of your annual worldwide and domestic GST/HST taxable supplies
  • fiscal year-end
  • effective date of registration
  • reporting period

You will also need all of the following if it differs from the basic business information above:

  • mailing address
  • physical address

RTNR - GST/HST for digital economy businesses

Information You Need for Registrations

  • Type of products or services the business collects GST/HST on and supplier type (if applicable)
  • Type of products or services the business provides in Canada
  • Description of the business operations in Canada
  • Products or services the business sells or provides in Canada that are exempt from GST/HST
  • Filing type (simplified or normal)
  • Canadian location where you store products (if applicable)
  • Information about the Canadian financial institution you use
  • Information about Canadian employees (if applicable)
  • Effective date of registration
  • Business fiscal year end
  • Expected amount of worldwide and Canadian sales for the next 12 months
  • Filing frequency

RP - Payroll deductions program account

You Must Have All of the Following On Hand:

  • Date employees receive their first wages
  • Months covered for payroll of employees' wages
  • Type of pay period (for example, weekly, quarterly)
  • Number of employees
  • Payroll service name (if any)
  • Country of parent or affiliate, if you have a foreign owned corporation
  • Name of franchisor (if any)
    • Country of franchise's head office

You Will Also Need All of the Following If It Differs from the Basic Business Information Above:

  • Mailing address
  • Physical address

RPNR - Non-res payroll

Information You Need for Registration:

  • Types of payments the business will be making
  • Frequency of payments
  • Expected number of employees
  • Date of first payment
  • Expected total of annual salaries for employees located in Canada
  • Duration of business activities
  • Subsidiary, affiliate or franchise information (if applicable)

RZ - Information returns program account

You Must Have All of the Following On Hand:

  • Type of program account:
    • T5 group
    • Tax-Free Savings Account (TFSA)
    • T5018
    • Partnerships
  • Effective date of registration

You Will Also Need All of the Following If It Differs from the Basic Business Information Above:

  • Mailing address
  • Physical address

RZNR - Non-res info returns

Information You Need for Registration:

  • Will you be filing certain Special Elections or Special Returns
  • Accounts you would like to apply for
  • Effective date of registration

RC - Corporation income tax program account

You Must Have All of the Following On Hand:

  • Corporation name
  • Certificate number
  • Date of incorporation
  • Jurisdiction

You Will Also Need All of the Following If It Differs from the Basic Business Information Above:

  • Mailing address
  • Physical address

RCNR - Non-res corporation

Information You Need for Registration:

  • Type of incorporated business
  • Date and country of incorporation
  • Certificate number
  • Will you be filing certain Special Elections or Special Returns?

RR - Registered charity program account

You Must Have All of the Following On Hand:

  • Type of program account:
    • Charity
    • Canadian Amateur Athletic Association (CAAA)
    • National Arts Service Organization (NASO)

You Will Also Need All of the Following If It Differs from the Basic Business Information Above:

  • Mailing address
  • Physical address
  • Books and records address

RU - Underused housing tax program account

You Will Have to Have the Following On Hand:

  • Type of account needed
  • Account effective date

You Will Also Need All of the Following If It Differs from the Basic Business Information Above:

  • Mailing address
  • Physical address
  • Books and records address

RUNR - Non-res business number

RUNR - Non-res UHT

  • Effective date of registration

LT - Luxury tax program account

  • Account effective date - only BRO
  • Registration type
    • Subject vehicles
    • ○ Subject aircraft
    • ○ Subject vessels
  • Business name and number
  • Physical/mailing/books and records address

CT - Fuel charge program account

Content

DST - Digital services tax

Information you need for registration:

  • Legal name
  • Business number (BN), if the business already has one
  • Date and place of incorporation and certificate number, if the business is incorporated
  • Physical address
  • Mailing address, if different from the physical address
  • Name and phone number of at least one officer or director
