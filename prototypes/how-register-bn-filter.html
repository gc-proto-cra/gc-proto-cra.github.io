Gather the following information to register for these accounts

BN - Business number

Personal information:

  • Last name
  • Social insurance number (SIN)
  • Date of birth
  • Personal postal code

If you are a representative, you will need to provide your RepID. For more information on representatives, go to Represent a Client.

Basic business information:

  • Business name
  • Type of business (for example: sole proprietor, partnership, corporation, domestic worker, trust)
  • Name and SIN of owners as required
  • Physical address
  • Mailing address (if different from the physical address)
  • Description of major business activity

RT - GST/HST program account

You must have all of the following on hand:

  • amount of your annual worldwide and domestic GST/HST taxable supplies
  • fiscal year-end
  • effective date of registration
  • reporting period

You will also need all of the following if it differs from the basic business information above:

  • mailing address
  • physical address

RP - Payroll deductions program account

You Must Have All of the Following On Hand:

  • Date employees receive their first wages
  • Months covered for payroll of employees' wages
  • Type of pay period (for example, weekly, quarterly)
  • Number of employees
  • Payroll service name (if any)
  • Country of parent or affiliate, if you have a foreign owned corporation
  • Name of franchisor (if any)
    • Country of franchise's head office

You Will Also Need All of the Following If It Differs from the Basic Business Information Above:

  • Mailing address
  • Physical address

RZ - Information returns program account

You Must Have All of the Following On Hand:

  • Type of program account:
    • T5 group
    • Tax-Free Savings Account (TFSA)
    • T5018
    • Partnerships
  • Effective date of registration

You Will Also Need All of the Following If It Differs from the Basic Business Information Above:

  • Mailing address
  • Physical address

RC - Corporation income tax program account

You Must Have All of the Following On Hand:

  • Corporation name
  • Certificate number
  • Date of incorporation
  • Jurisdiction

You Will Also Need All of the Following If It Differs from the Basic Business Information Above:

  • Mailing address
  • Physical address

RR - Registered charity program account

You Must Have All of the Following On Hand:

  • Type of program account:
    • Charity
    • Canadian Amateur Athletic Association (CAAA)
    • National Arts Service Organization (NASO)

You Will Also Need All of the Following If It Differs from the Basic Business Information Above:

  • Mailing address
  • Physical address
  • Books and records address

RU - Underused housing tax program account

You Will Have to Have the Following On Hand:

  • Type of account needed
  • Account effective date

You Will Also Need All of the Following If It Differs from the Basic Business Information Above:

  • Mailing address
  • Physical address
  • Books and records address

LT - Luxury tax program account

  • Account effective date - only BRO
  • Registration type
    • Subject vehicles
    • ○ Subject aircraft
    • ○ Subject vessels
  • Business name and number
  • Physical/mailing/books and records address

CT - Fuel charge program account

Content

DST - Digital services tax

Information you need for registration:

  • Legal name
  • Business number (BN), if the business already has one
  • Date and place of incorporation and certificate number, if the business is incorporated
  • Physical address
  • Mailing address, if different from the physical address
  • Name and phone number of at least one officer or director

Choose the method that works best for you

Business Registration Online

Processing time: Immediate

This is the simplest way to complete your registration.

You can also use this option to get a business number and add certain other program accounts in the same registration session.

What you can do in BRO at the same time

You can do the following at the same time in BRO:

  • Get a business number
  • Add one or more of the following program accounts:
    • GST/HST
    • Payroll deductions
    • Corporation income tax
    • Registered charity
    • Information returns
    • Luxury tax
    • Underused housing tax

When you register, remember to:

  • Enter your information in a timely manner because the form will time out after 30 minutes of inactivity
    • to get a
  • Complete the form in one session because you cannot save information during the registration process

If you're a business owner

You can enter BRO through one of the following methods:

  • BRO quick link: Option 1

    You will have to add your SIN and other identifying information

    Register now

  • Your CRA account: Option 2

    On the Welcome page, select Add account, then select Business account, then select Business Registration Online

    Sign in to your CRA account

If you're a representative with a RepID

Enter BRO through Represent a Client in your CRA account.

Your CRA account

Processing time: Info

  1. Fill out Form L500, Luxury Tax Registration Application
    If you also need a business number, fill out Form RC1, Request for a Business Number and Certain Program Accounts
    Additional forms you may need to fill out when registering

    If you would like to apply to keep books and records outside Canada, an authorization under subsection 88(3) of the Select Luxury Items Tax Act may be granted to maintain such records outside Canada. To request this authorization, you must fill out form L500-1, Non-Resident – Records Kept Outside Canada. 

    If you would like to apply to file separate returns for different branches or divisions of your business under subsection 73(1) of the Select Luxury Items Tax Act, you must fill out form L500-2, Application or Revocation of the Authorization to File Separate Luxury Tax Returns for Branches and Divisions

  2. Sign in to your CRA account
  3. Use the Submit documents function in My Business Account
  4. Select the option to upload a document without a case or reference number
  5. In the list of possible reasons for your submission, choose Luxury Tax, Fuel Charge, Excise Duty, Excise Tax and ATSC
  6. Select Submit Luxury Tax registration forms or supporting document
  7. Choose and attach your saved form
By mail

Processing time: Info

  1. Fill out Form L500, Luxury Tax Registration Application
    If you also need a business number, fill out Form RC1, Request for a Business Number and Certain Program Accounts
    Additional forms you may need to fill out when registering

    If you would like to apply to keep books and records outside Canada, an authorization under subsection 88(3) of the Select Luxury Items Tax Act may be granted to maintain such records outside Canada. To request this authorization, you must fill out form L500-1, Non-Resident – Records Kept Outside Canada. 

    If you would like to apply to file separate returns for different branches or divisions of your business under subsection 73(1) of the Select Luxury Items Tax Act, you must fill out form L500-2, Application or Revocation of the Authorization to File Separate Luxury Tax Returns for Branches and Divisions

  2. Send your completed forms to the appropriate address listed on the last page of the forms.
