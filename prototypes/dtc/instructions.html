Instructions

During the test, the moderator will present you with a number of different tasks, one at a time. Once you have read and understand the task, click the “start task” button. This will open a website in a new tab. Use the website to try to complete the task. When you are done, come back to the tab where the task is and select your answer. After selecting your answer for each task, you will respond to two questions about the task you just completed.

As you go through these tasks, please don't use the internal site search or Google to find the answers. Use the Canada Revenue Agency website to find the answers to the questions asked of you. We are testing the web pages, not your knowledge. If you already know the answer, please try to find the answer on the website.

Please remember to tell us what you’re thinking and how you are navigating as you go through the scenarios.
