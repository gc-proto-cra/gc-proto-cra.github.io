Additional resources for employees: CRA and COVID-19

Information to support employees during COVID-19 relating to your pay, health and safety, worksite informaton and other topics.

Your health and safety

Your health and safety is our number one priority. Below you will find information to ensure a safe work environment, whether at home or at a worksite.

  • Employee Assistance Program (EAP)
  • Temporary Public Service Health Care Plan changes

To find more information about health and safety, such as accommodation measures, ergonomics, or reporting workplace injury, illness, and hazards, visit InfoZone or speak with your manager.

Your mental health

Protect your mental health, get help, balance work and personal responsibilities, support employees and teams

  • Mental health tips for working from home
  • Self-Care and Resilience Guide
  • Government of Canada’s Mental Health and COVID-19 information hub
  • The Importance of Self-Care during the COVID-19 Pandemic
  • Self-care tips for managers and their teams during the COVID-19

For more tips about self-care and taking care of your mental health, visit InfoZone or talk to your manager.

Your rights and responsibilities

Find out about your rights and responsibilities as an employee and/or as a manager by visiting Your rights and responsibilities.

Provincial and Territorial COVID-19 Guidelines

As the economy reopens across the country, provinces and territories are each taking precautions to ensure a healthy and safe environment for all. Follow the Provincial and territorial resources for COVID-19 for the latest updates on your province or territory of residence.
