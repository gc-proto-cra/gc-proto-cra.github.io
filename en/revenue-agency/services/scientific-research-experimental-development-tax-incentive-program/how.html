How to prepare your SR&ED claim

Follow the steps below to prepare your SR&ED tax incentives claim yourself.

Steps

  1. Get ready to claim

    Understand how to claim, resources you’ll need, due dates, and which records to save

  2. Group work into SR&ED projects

    Review what an SR&ED project is and how to group your eligible work into projects

  3. Report expenditures

    Review how to determine your SR&ED expenditures and use the SR&ED Expenditures Claim form

  4. Calculate allowable expenditures

    Choose the proxy or traditional method and determine your allowable expenditures (salary or wages, materials, contracts, overhead, and third-party payments)

  5. Calculate pool of deductibles

    Determine your pool of deductible SR&ED expenditures

  6. Calculate qualified expenditures

    Determine the SR&ED expenditures that qualify for an investment tax credit (ITC)

  7. Get an investment tax credit (ITC)

    Learn who can get an ITC and which form to use, review rates, and find out if your ITC is refundable
