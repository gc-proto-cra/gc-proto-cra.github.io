Scientific research and experimental development (SR&ED) tax incentives

Greater Toronto Area West Tax Services Office information webinars

The Greater Toronto Area West Tax Services Office serves businesses that conduct scientific research and experimental development (SR&ED) work in Ontario.

Free information webinars provide a general overview of the SR&ED program, and explain the eligibility criteria, what expenditures are eligible, and how to file an SR&ED claim.

The webinar will focus on Ontario, however anyone interested in the SR&ED program may attend.

Public webinars on SR&ED financial issues

This information webinar provides a summary of the financial aspects of the SR&ED program. It is intended for financial or accounting staff already familiar with the basics of the SR&ED program. Attendees will be taken through the completion of a T661 Form and the calculation of investment tax credits.
