Make a payment - Personal income tax

Payment options for the following:

  • Personal taxes (T1 – individual income tax) payments
  • COVID-19 benefit repayments
  • Benefits and credits repayments (such as CCB, GST/HST, CPP, EI, OAS)
  • Part XIII – non-resident withholding tax for individuals

Choose an option to pay what you owe:

Online
Online banking
Description
Make a payment or schedule future payments to the CRA through your Canadian financial institution
Processing time
Typically 1 to 3 business days
Details

Pay by online banking

My Payment (Interac®, Visa Debit®, Debit Mastercard®)
Description
My Payment is a service that allows you to pay directly to the CRA using a debit card (does not include credit cards)
Processing time
Typically same or next business day
Details

Pay by debit online with My Payment

Pre-authorized debit (PAD)
Description
Authorize the CRA to withdraw amounts from your Canadian bank account on the dates you choose
Processing time
Typically 1 to 3 business days
Details

Pay by pre-authorized debit online
