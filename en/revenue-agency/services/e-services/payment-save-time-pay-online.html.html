Pay now with My Payment

My Payment is an electronic service that lets you make payments directly to the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) using your bank access card.

You cannot use credit cards with My Payment. For credit card payment options, go to Pay by credit card, PayPal, or Interac e-Transfer.

To use My Payment, you need an activated debit card with one or more of the following logos:

Pay now

Ask your financial institution about your transaction limit and any fees they may charge for making online payments. The CRA does not charge a fee for using this service.

How long it takes

Payments are usually received by the CRA the same day or next business day. To avoid fees and interest, please make sure you pay on time.

Hours of service

My Payment is available 21 hours a day, seven days a week.

Other ways to pay

Available payment methods depend on the type of payment you are making. See Make a payment.

Endorsements

The CRA does not endorse websites, companies, products, services, or publications. They are provided for convenience only.
