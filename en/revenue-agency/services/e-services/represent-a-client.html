Represent a Client

New My Trust Account portal for representatives

The CRA has launched My Trust Account, a secure portal that lets legal and authorized representatives of trust accounts manage trust information online. You can securely access My Trust Account services through Represent a Client.

For more information, visit About My Trust Account.

Process for confirming a representative

Authorization requests submitted through Represent a Client may need to be confirmed by the client from one of the CRA’s sign-in services. To learn more about the process and some exceptions, go to Confirm my representative’s authorization request - Canada.ca.

If your CRA user ID and password have been revoked

Some taxpayers may have received a notification that their CRA user ID and password have been revoked. Visit CRA user ID and password have been revoked for more information.

The manage direct deposit service is currently unavailable

The manage direct deposit service is unavailable at this time. We regret the inconvenience. Here is another way to manage your direct deposit information.
