Business number and CRA program accounts

When you register your business, you get a unique 9-digit business number (BN) that identifies your business. Your BN is used to interact with provincial or territorial, municipal, and other federal programs.

You can only have one BN for your business. If you need a program account, such as GST/HST or Payroll, these accounts are added to your BN.
