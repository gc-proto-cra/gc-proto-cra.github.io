GST/HST for businesses

The CRA will waive interest and penalties for businesses in these postal codes affected by wildfires in British Columbia and the Northwest Territories. This is applicable for all GST/HST and T2 returns and payments due from August 15 to October 16, 2023 inclusive. Returns and payments must be submitted by October 16.
