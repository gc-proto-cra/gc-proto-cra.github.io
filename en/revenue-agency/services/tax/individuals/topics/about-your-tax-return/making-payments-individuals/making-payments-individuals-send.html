The CRA only accepts payment for defaulted federal student/apprentice loans or allowance overpayments that are in collections, on behalf of Employment and Social Development Canada (ESDC).

The CRA only accepts payment for overpayment of benefits and assistance that are in collections, on behalf of Employment and Social Development Canada (ESDC).

The CRA only accepts payment for custom import debts that are in collections, on behalf of Canada Border Service Agency (CBSA).
