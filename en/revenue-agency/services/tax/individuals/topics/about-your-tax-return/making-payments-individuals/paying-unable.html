Call us to discuss your situation

The Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) is committed to putting people first and providing the income support that Canadians need during the COVID-19 pandemic.

You must call the CRA to discuss possible options:

Calling us from within Canada and the United States

You can complete a personal tax debt call request to receive a callback from a CRA agent or locate the call topic and corresponding phone number from the tables below.

Consequences of not paying

If you ignore your tax responsibilities and debt, or refuse to co-operate with the CRA to eliminate your debt, the CRA may take legal action.

For details: Consequences of not paying your personal tax debt

Options if you cannot pay your debt

Options if you cannot pay your debt
Situation Summary More information
Debt deferral If your inability to pay is temporary, contact us to discuss deferring repayment until you are financially able to start a payment arrangement. Contact us
Taxpayer relief You may ask for relief from penalties and interest to reduce the amount you or your business owes. Taxpayer relief provisions
Insolvency or bankruptcy If you have no other financial options, you might consider insolvency or bankruptcy Office of the superintendent of bankruptcy
