Payment methods

You can pay your tax instalments online, by mail, or in person. Choose a payment option and check processing times.

For details: Make a payment

Remittance vouchers for payments made in person or by mail

If you are paying by mail, or in person at your financial institution or at a Canada Post location, you will need to submit your payment along with your remittance voucher.

Your instalment remittance voucher (form INNS3) will be included in the instalment reminder package that we mailed to you, unless you pay your instalments by pre-authorized debit.

If you need more remittance vouchers (form INNS3):

Online
  • My Account
  • Represent a Client
  • Download a fillable remittance vouchers
By phone
  • Call our automated TIPS line at 1-800-267-6999

The CRA will charge a fee, if your financial institution does not honour your payment, including stop-payments.

After you make a payment

You can verify in My Account if the payment has been applied to your online balance. We will not immediately send you a receipt, so you should keep proof of your instalment payment until you receive Instalment Payment Summary (form INNS2), or until the payment has been applied in My Account.

If you overpaid

You can ask for a refund of an overpayment of your instalments by sending a letter to your tax centre or by calling 1-800-959-8281. We will review the matter on a case-by-case basis.
