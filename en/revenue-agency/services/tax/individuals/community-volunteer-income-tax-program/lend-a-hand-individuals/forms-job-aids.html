Forms and job aids

The table includes forms and job aids volunteers use when preparing tax returns through the CVITP.

Note

These documents are not available on Canada.ca. For a complete list of forms, visit Canada Revenue Agency forms listed by number.

Click on the name to access the PDF version that you may view, print or save.

Note 1: By default, you should use the form generated by the tax software. However, if you are using a hard copy, it is very important to add the expiry date. This is the same expiry date you would see on the electronic version. Remember, the Authorization request - signature page is not to be sent to the CRA.
