Installing the software

If you are using your own computer to prepare tax returns for the CVITP, you will get an email from your organization with an activation key.

If you need to prepare a tax return for a prior year, you can download it from UFile CVITP. You will need to inform the contact person at your community organization, or your CRA coordinator, so you can also obtain the separate activation key needed for each year’s version of the UFile software.

Nice to know

Contact your CRA coordinator for assistance if you are unable to download the software directly from the UFile website.

UFile CVITP system requirements:

  • Operating system: Windows® 10 is required for the proper operation of the UFile CVITP software
    • Macintosh (Apple) operating systems are not compatible with the UFile CVITP software
  • Hard disk space: 250mb available
  • Display: Video card and monitor capable of 1024x768 resolution at 256 colours (16-bit colour recommended)
  • Internet access is required in order to use EFILE and obtain the latest software updates

UFile CVITP software will display the following logo in the upper right corner of each screen:

Text version

Logo of UFile software: half of a white maple leaf on red background and text UFile CVITP.

Follow these steps if the UFile CVITP logo is not displayed:

  • complete the activation
  • start UFile
  • click settings (above the logo)
  • select EFILE-NetFile setup
  • in the field In what capacity are you preparing tax returns?
    • select Volunteer with the CVITP
  • click Save, at the bottom
    • pop-up windows appears
    • click OK

The UFile CVITP logo should now appear in the upper right-hand corner.

UFile CVITP welcome screen

Once UFile CVITP has loaded, it will open to the welcome screen.

Text version

UFile 2021 screen
Start tab is highlighted

To move from one section to another, use the tabs along the top menu bar: Start, Interview, Review, Tax Return, and EFILE.

On this screen

You will be able to navigate to the Review, Tax Return, or EFILE tabs only if you enter all required fields, marked with red asterisks (*), in the Interview.

The search feature (magnifying glass icon), help menu (question mark), and settings menu (gear icon) are located in the top-right corner.

The search feature allows you to find information about a specific topic. For example, if you type ‘spousal amount’ into the search bar, a pop-up window opens listing all topics that include references to the spousal amount.

The help menu provides links to the most common topics, as well as an alphabetical index of all topics.

Nice to know

You may also access the help menu by clicking on New Tutorial.

The settings menu allows you to Check for updates, Register the software, and complete the EFILE-NetFile setup.

To begin a new tax return, click Start on a new tax file.

EFILE-NetFile setup

In order to electronically file (EFILE) tax returns, you must complete the EFILE-NetFile setup. The EFILE-NetFile setup becomes available during the installation process. You can also access it by clicking the settings menu (gear icon).

For more information on EFILE, refer to the CRA electronic services section.

To complete EFILE setup:

  • open UFile CVITP 2021
  • click on settings, found at the top right corner, and select EFILE-NetFile setup
    • Volunteer with the CVITP will be preselected in the drop-down menu under the question In what capacity are you preparing tax returns?
    • EFILE has automatically been chosen as the electronic filing method

    Important note

    If  Volunteer with the CVITP, and EFILE are not the default options, you are not using the CVITP version of UFile.

    You need to go back and carefully follow the UFile CVITP software installation instructions.

  • complete both Part A and Part B of the EFILE registration information section:
    • make sure the following fields, marked with red asterisks (*), are all entered
      • your CRA EFILE number
      • your CRA EFILE password
      • your name and address
      • the organization name and city
      • the COIN number, if your organization provided you with their COIN
    • Yes is ticked automatically for the question, Do you have a Coin?
    • if you do not have a COIN, tick No and proceed as normal
    • if you do not include a COIN, you will still be able to prepare and transmit tax returns

      Important note

      Although not entering a COIN still allows you to continue with preparing the tax return, be aware that entering an invalid COIN will generate an error message that will prevent your settings from saving.

    • click Save

    If you are working on a computer that is used by other volunteers within the same community organization, you must open the EFILE-NetFile setup and ensure that your EFILE number, password and COIN (if available) are all entered correctly before you complete any tax returns.

    Important info

    When you finish using the computer, remember to remove your identification information from Part A of the EFILE-NetFile setup page by clicking on Clear Part A, and then Save. Doing this will allow the information for the business/organization to still remain in Part B. However, if you need to delete all information, including the business/organization, click Clear All.
