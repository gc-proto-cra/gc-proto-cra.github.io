Provincial and territorial benefits and credits

When you prepare an individual’s tax return, it is important to familiarize yourself with both the federal and provincial benefits and credits for which they may be eligible.

Use the following resources on Canada.ca to find more information on the benefits and credits for the province or territory in which the individual lives.

Resources

  • Benefit finder  – search for benefits in the individual’s province or territory; for more information, call 1‑800‑O‑Canada (1‑800‑622‑6232)
  • Provincial and territorial tax and credits for individuals – search for provincial and territorial credits related to eligible individuals
  • Provincial and territorial programs – search for information on provincial or territorial programs administered by the CRA
  • Provincial credits and grants you can claim on your tax return – search for information related to credits and grants for homeowners offered in some provinces
