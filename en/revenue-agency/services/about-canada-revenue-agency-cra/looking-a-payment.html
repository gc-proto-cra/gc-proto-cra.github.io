Confirm your payment is received - Payments to the CRA

If you make a payment to the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA), it may not be applied to your account right away.

Before confirming your payment has been processed by the CRA, allow:

  • 3 business days for online payments
  • 10 business days for payments by cheque

Ways to confirm your payment was received:

Individuals

View details in your CRA My Account under "Accounts and payments".

Call us if your payment was not applied to an account as expected:

  • Canadian residents: 1-800-959-8281
  • Non-residents
    • Canada or U.S.: 1-855-284-5946
    • Outside Canada or U.S.: 1-613-940-8499

To verify your identity, you will need to provide additional information, such as details from an assessed tax return.

Businesses

View in My Business Account under "View and pay account balance".

If your payment was not applied to your account as expected, you can:

  • use My Business Account or Represent a Client to transfer payments between your various accounts
  • call 1-800-959-5525

To verify your identity, you will need to provide additional information, such as details from an assessed tax return.
