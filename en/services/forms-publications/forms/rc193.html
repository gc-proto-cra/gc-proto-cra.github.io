RC193 Service Feedback

You may be looking for:

  • Send feedback about CRA service
  • Submit accessibility feedback

Use the RC193 Service Feedback form to submit feedback about your service experience with the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA).

Select a form option to:

  • Send a service complaint, suggestion or compliment
  • Discuss an accessibility barrier or the CRA Accessibility Plan

Download instructions for fillable PDFs

You must download the accessible fillable PDF to your computer. Use Acrobat Reader 10 or later to open the file. Do not click the link to open it in your web browser.

  1. Download and save the PDF to your computer
  2. Open the downloaded PDF in Acrobat Reader 10 or later

Ways to get the form

  • Use the online form

    • Submit service feedback (web version)

  • Download and fill out with Acrobat Reader

    You must download and open fillable PDFs in Acrobat Reader 10 or higher.

    • Accessible Fillable PDF (rc193-fill-22e.pdf)

  • Print and fill out by hand

    • Standard print PDF (rc193-22e.pdf)
    • Large print PDF (rc193-lp-22e.pdf)

  • Ask for an alternate format

    You can order alternate formats such as digital audio, electronic text, braille, and large print.

    • Order alternate formats for persons with disabilities
