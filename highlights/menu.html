COVID campaign page highlight reel

When watching the videos for user testing there are often many interactions and observances that are interesting and useful for content designers but not really captured by any deliverables. This website aims to bridge that gap by presenting segments our UX team found interesting as well as the notes to go along with them.

Task 1

Sarah stopped working on March 30, 2020 because of the pandemic. Find a program that is available for Sarah to get immediate financial help if she has lost her income.

Start page: Changes to taxes and benefits (landing page)

Correct answer: CERB

  1. Sarah should look into the Canada Emergency Response Benefit
  2. Sarah should look into the Canada Emergency Wage Subsidy
  3. Sarah should look into the Canada Emergency Student Benefit
  4. I couldn't find the answer
