COVID-19 Campaign Site

Rapid UX design and research

The UX R&D Panther Matrix team led the user experience (UX) design and testing for the CRA's COVID-19 key campaign pages. Through a series of iterative baseline and validation tests, and rapid prototyping, the team optimized the usability and task performance of the campaign pages, and redesigned the users' experience.
